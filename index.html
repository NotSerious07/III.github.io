BMW

Sheer Driving Pleasure — Since 1916

History

BMW (Bayerische Motoren Werke AG) was founded in 1916, originally producing aircraft engines. Over the decades, BMW has become one of the most iconic premium automobile manufacturers, blending engineering excellence with luxury and sportiness.

Innovation

Electric Future

The BMW i Series pioneers sustainable driving with cutting-edge electric technology.

Engineering Precision

BMW’s inline-six engines are a benchmark in smoothness and performance.

Driver-Centric Design

Every BMW cockpit is angled towards the driver — combining ergonomics with style.

Motorsport

BMW has a rich motorsport legacy, from touring cars to Formula 1 and endurance racing. The M Division translates this racing DNA into high-performance road cars.
